04 — Applications

Real-World
LLM Applications

✍️
Content Creation & Generation
Automating articles, marketing copy, social media posts, and creative writing. LLMs serve as powerful co-authors — accelerating ideation, drafting, and editing workflows at scale.
🎧
Customer Service & Support
Intelligent chatbots and virtual assistants delivering 24/7 support, resolving complex queries in natural language, and routing escalations — dramatically reducing response times and costs.
💻
Code Generation & Assistance
Helping developers write, debug, review, and document code more efficiently. Tools like GitHub Copilot and Cursor suggest completions, explain unfamiliar codebases, and generate test suites.
🔬
Scientific & Medical Research
Accelerating literature review, hypothesis generation, and clinical decision support. Domain-specific LLMs (Med-PaLM, BioGPT) are transforming how researchers navigate vast knowledge bases.
📊
Enterprise Knowledge Management
RAG-powered internal search, document Q&A, and knowledge base assistants that let employees query company documents, policies, and reports in natural language — without hallucination.
🌐
Multilingual & Cross-lingual AI
Breaking language barriers through machine translation, cross-lingual retrieval, and multilingual chatbots. Modern LLMs enable fluent communication and information access across dozens of languages.
05 — Ethics

Responsible LLMs &
Societal Impact

The power of large language models comes with serious responsibility. This course treats ethics not as an afterthought but as a core technical and intellectual discipline — integrated across the curriculum, with a dedicated final module on responsible deployment at scale.

⚠️
Bias and Fairness
LLMs inherit and can amplify biases embedded in pretraining data — affecting hiring tools, medical advice systems, and content moderation. We study detection methods, de-biasing techniques at the data, training, and inference levels, and the structural limits of purely technical fixes.
🔒
Data Privacy and Consent
LLMs trained on web-scale data raise critical questions about data privacy, memorisation of personal information, and extraction attacks. We cover GDPR compliance, ethical data sourcing, differential privacy, and the risks of training data regurgitation.
🎭
Hallucination and Misinformation
LLMs can generate confident but factually incorrect outputs — a phenomenon with serious consequences in medical, legal, and educational contexts. We analyse hallucination causes, grounding techniques (RAG, RLHF), and evaluation frameworks for factual accuracy.
🌱
Environmental Sustainability
Training and serving frontier LLMs carries enormous computational and carbon costs. We discuss efficiency research (PEFT, distillation, quantisation), responsible compute reporting, and the imperative for sustainable scaling practices in both academia and industry.
