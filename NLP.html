04 — Applications

Real-World
NLP Applications

🌐
Machine Translation
Automatically translating text or speech across languages, breaking down communication barriers. Powered by neural sequence-to-sequence models and multilingual transformers.
💬
Sentiment Analysis
Determining the emotional tone of text at scale — used for customer feedback analysis, brand monitoring, and social media intelligence across languages and domains.
🤖
Chatbots & Assistants
Conversational AI systems that understand and respond to natural language queries — from customer service automation to AI tutoring agents and RAG-powered knowledge systems.
📋
Information Extraction
Automatically identifying structured information — entities, relations, events — from unstructured text. Core to search engines, biomedical NLP, and financial intelligence.
🚫
Spam & Content Filtering
Identifying and filtering malicious or harmful text content at scale — from email spam detection to hate speech moderation and content safety classification.
✏️
Writing Assistance
Grammar and style checking, text completion, essay evaluation — NLP tools that augment human writing. Includes automated assessment systems for educational settings.
05 — Ethics

Responsible NLP &
Societal Impact

As NLP technologies become more powerful and widely deployed, it is essential to engage critically with their societal effects. This course treats ethics not as a peripheral concern but as a core technical and intellectual discipline — woven throughout all twelve weeks.

⚠️
Algorithmic Bias in Text Data
NLP models trained on biased corpora can perpetuate and amplify societal stereotypes — in hiring tools, translation systems, and emotion classifiers. We study detection methods, debiasing techniques, and structural causes of representation harm.
🔒
Privacy & Data Security
Processing sensitive personal information in NLP applications raises fundamental privacy concerns. This includes differential privacy, anonymisation, GDPR compliance, and the particular challenges of training data memorisation in large language models.
🎭
Misinformation & Synthetic Text
Advanced text generation can create convincing misinformation and synthetic media. We examine detection methods, the arms race between generation and detection, and the societal responsibilities of NLP researchers and practitioners.
🌱
Environmental Sustainability
Training large language models carries significant computational and carbon costs. We discuss efficient model design, knowledge distillation, and the imperative for sustainable AI research practices and reporting.
